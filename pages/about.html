Our Story

JesLights Tours & Travel was founded by **Jesca Kinya**, a passionate advocate for sustainable tourism and a proud native of East Africa. Growing up amidst the breathtaking landscapes and vibrant cultures of Kenya, Jesca developed a deep appreciation for her homeland and a desire to share its wonders with the world.

Driven by a vision to offer authentic, responsible, and accessible travel experiences, Jesca established JesLights. Our journey began with a commitment to not only showcase the iconic wildlife and stunning natural beauty of East Africa but also to foster meaningful connections between travelers and local communities, ensuring that tourism benefits everyone involved.

Our Vision & Mission

Our Vision

To be the leading and most trusted tour operator in East Africa, renowned for delivering exceptional, authentic, and sustainable travel experiences that inspire and enrich lives.

Our Mission

To craft personalized and unforgettable journeys through East Africa, prioritizing customer satisfaction, promoting responsible tourism, and empowering local communities through genuine cultural exchange.
