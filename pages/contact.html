Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a tour?

You can book a tour directly through our website by navigating to the "Tours" page, selecting your desired tour, and clicking the "Book Now" button. Follow the prompts to complete your booking. Alternatively, you can contact our team for assistance.

What is included in the tour price?

Each tour's inclusions and exclusions are clearly listed on its individual tour detail page. Generally, our tours include accommodation, meals as specified, park entrance fees, professional guides, and transportation. International flights and visa fees are usually excluded.

Do you offer custom tours?

Yes, we specialize in creating personalized tour itineraries. If you have specific destinations, activities, or dates in mind, please contact us with your requirements, and our travel experts will design a tailor-made adventure for you.

What are your sustainability practices?

JesLights is deeply committed to sustainable tourism. We partner with eco-friendly lodges, support local communities through direct engagement and fair practices, minimize environmental impact, and educate our travelers on responsible tourism.
